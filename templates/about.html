About Our Food Donation Initiative

Our mission is to reduce food waste and support communities in need by facilitating food donations from hotels. We connect hotels with surplus food to volunteers who help deliver these donations to local shelters, food banks, and those in need. By working together, we aim to create a sustainable and impactful solution to food waste.

Hotels can post details about leftover food items, and volunteers can view and accept these donations through our platform. This collaboration not only minimizes waste but also fosters a sense of community and compassion.
