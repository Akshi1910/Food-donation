Welcome to the Waste Food Management and Donation System

This platform connects hotels and volunteers to reduce food waste and ensure leftover food reaches those in need. Hotels can post available food items, and volunteers can accept donations or request food for distribution.

About Us

The Waste Food Management and Donation System aims to bridge the gap between surplus food from hotels and the needs of the community. By encouraging food donation, we reduce waste and support local communities.
